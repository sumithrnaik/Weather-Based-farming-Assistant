Our Goal

At Weather-based Farming AI Assistant, our mission is to empower farmers with technology that enhances decision-making and improves agricultural efficiency. We aim to help farmers maximize crop yields, reduce water usage, and mitigate risks associated with changing weather conditions.

Project Features

Real-time Weather Data: Our platform integrates with a reliable weather API to fetch real-time weather data, including current temperature, humidity, and weather conditions, along with a 7-day forecast. This information is crucial for making informed farming decisions.

Crop Recommendations: Using a sophisticated machine learning model, we provide personalized crop recommendations based on soil nutrients (N, P, K), temperature, humidity, pH levels, and rainfall. Our model is trained to suggest the most suitable crops for your specific soil and environmental conditions.

Irrigation Advice: Our AI-powered irrigation model takes weather data into account to offer precise irrigation advice. By predicting the exact amount of water needed (in mm), we help farmers optimize water usage, ensuring crops receive the right amount of hydration without wastage.

How It Works

Weather Data Integration: Our platform fetches real-time weather data from a trusted weather API, providing up-to-date information on current weather conditions and a 7-day forecast.

User Inputs: Farmers input essential parameters such as soil nutrient levels (N, P, K), temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall into the platform.

Machine Learning Models: Our trained machine learning models process the weather data and user inputs to predict the best crops for cultivation and provide precise irrigation advice.

Recommendations and Alerts: The platform displays crop recommendations and irrigation advice enabling farmers to make data-driven decisions for their agricultural practices.
